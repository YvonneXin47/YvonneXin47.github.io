Golden Trust Archery

Golden Trust Archery Club was founded in Oct, 2012. It is the 1st archery training centre in HK since 2012. We are keen to promote archery in Hong Kong. We are also the biggest archery centre in Hong Kong, with 7000 sq feet and 25 shooting lanes. Our location is just 3 mins from Lai Chi Kok MTR. Come and share the joy of archery.

Lion Rock Archery Club

The Lion Rock Archery Club was established in 1988 as a non-profit organization of the Hong Kong Archery Association. It aims to promote the archery culture and provide members with training and practice opportunities. It is named after the Lion Rock Park as the club is located in the park and it is one of the few archery clubs with its own practice range in Hong Kong.

Hong Kong Archery Workshop

Tuen Mun Archery Range

The Tuen Mun Archery Range occupies a site of 4700 sq metres next to the Tuen Mun Public Riding School. It was open for public use on 1 May 1995. This facility can serve as an archery range with a shooting distance of 70 metres long for groups or individual hiring. In addition, the Centre organizes archery training courses and archery competition recreational activities regularly for public participation.

  54 Lung Mun Rd, Tuen Mun

Ngau Chi Wan Park Archery Range

Ngau Chi Wan Park Archery Range, occupying an area of 5500 square metres, is situated on the Eastern Platform of the Park. The Archery Range can be set up with a shooting distance of up to 90 metres. Archery training courses and competitions are regularly organised for public participation.
