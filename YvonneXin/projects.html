CareIT

Intro: Smart Healthcare App for HK Elderly (Conceptual Work)
Competition: 2019 HKBU Data & Media Hack
Prize: 1st Runner Up (the second prize)
Time: 2019.01.25-01.26

Background: Data and Media Hack 2019 is a competition that sets the stage for innovation, calling on teams consisting of students with mentors from various backgrounds to address social or commercial issues that matter to our future. Our team focused on providing innovative solutions to the prominent ageing issues in Hong Kong.

Introduction: CareIT is a smart healthcare application aiming to support people in their later years of life. The concept is by combining IoT and AI technologies to offer users with health status monitoring, automatic emergency alert and AI powered health record analysis services.

Responsibility: Contributing to product conceptualization with my knowledge in product design and management; Supporting the pitching by building a website for product value declaration and product demonstration.
