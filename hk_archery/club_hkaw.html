Hong Kong Archery Workshop

The Hong Kong Archery Workshop is founded in 2003. Located on Peace Avenue in Mong Kok, it includes an equipment store, a workshop for equipment maintenance and customization, and a practice range.


  1/F 10-12 Fu Tao Building, 98 Argyle Street, Mong Kok

  2782 3528

The HK Archery website is designed to serve the local archery amateurs and to engage more people with this sport!
